Privacy Policy for BIMetic Saved Viewpoints Manager

BIMetic ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by BIMetic.

This Privacy Policy applies to our application, BIMetic Saved Viewpoints Manager, and its associated services. By accessing or using our Service, you signify that you have read, understood, and agree to our collection, storage, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Our application functions primarily locally on your desktop within the Autodesk Navisworks environment. However, to provide licensing and software updates, we may collect:

  1. Authentication Data: When you log into Autodesk Navisworks, our plugin uses the Autodesk Entitlement API to verify your license. We only access your unique, anonymized Autodesk User ID to send a secure verification request to Autodesk's servers.
  2. Local Snapshots: The plugin's Backup & Restore feature saves JSON snapshots of your viewpoints structure locally on your machine at %LOCALAPPDATA%\BIMeticWebView2\Backups. We do NOT have access to these files.
  3. Usage Telemetry (Optional): If enabled, we may collect anonymous, non-identifying telemetry (such as the total number of viewpoints processed) to help us improve the software's performance.

We DO NOT collect, store, or transmit your actual Navisworks 3D models, proprietary clash data, or viewpoint contents to our servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Verify your subscription and entitlement to use the software via the Autodesk App Store.
  • Provide, operate, and maintain our application.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our application based on usage trends.
  • Understand and analyze how you use our application to fix bugs.

Security of Your Information

We take the security of your data very seriously. Because our application operates primarily offline within your local desktop environment, your proprietary engineering and BIM data never leaves your machine.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page or through the Autodesk App Store update notes.
